## Rationale for Selection

Given the 20-page constraint, I prioritized:
1. **Foundational frameworks** (Lasswell, Larson, Druckman) that structure the entire argument
2. **Key theories** (Social Identity, Social Learning) central to demonstrations
3. **Novel contributions** (IC standards application to academia)
4. **Essential datasets** (Kunst et al.)
5. **Distinguishing comparisons** (Park & Vezhnevets for ABM contrast)

Excluded citations that were:
- Historical context or background
- Technical surveys that don't add to main argument
- Example theories mentioned in passing
- Duplicate references
- Methodological tangents

This selective approach maintains academic rigor while respecting space constraints. The 12 citations provide essential grounding without cluttering the text with excessive references that would require compensating text reduction.
